The Highest Quality PPE

As a major distributor of medical supplies, our effort is built on an entirely self-reliant supply chain designed to create sustainable and scalable solutions for the highest quality PPE for US medical and civilian use.

OUR FOCUS IS USA-FIRST

We strive to source mostly MADE IN USA products that helps the local economy. For products that cannot be sourced locally, we securely source products from overseas manufacturers.

Leverage Our Relationships With Manufacturers

We understand how daunting sourcing from overseas can be. But our deep understanding of the supply chain and our direct relationships with the manufacturers, mitigates risks associated with international trade, logistics and distribution.
